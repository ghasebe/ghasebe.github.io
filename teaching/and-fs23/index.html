My exercise classes take place on Mondays at 9:15 in ETZ H91. I teach in German.
In the Example Lesson tab you can see how I try to structure my exercise classes.

Please submit the solutions to the weekly assignments by Monday 9:15 and the peergrading by Tuesday 23:59. One solution per group is sufficient.
